Our History

  • 1967

    Timeliners started their journey. First store opened at Coldstream Avenue. Started with 5 managers and reached here.

  • 1975

    Established new store at the Bruce Avenue with 20 New employees. Won best service providers in the town for 1975.

  • 2004

    Made business International. Opened stores in India, USA and London. Started Online store on the company's website.

  • 2018

    Won Best Service Providers and having best work environment awards in the Province. Started selling Rolex watches.
